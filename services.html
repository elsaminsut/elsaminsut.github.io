Services

Planning services for facade installations

Creation of optimized module layouts for solar building skins, finding the best choice based on cost-efficiency, energy yield, and visual appeal. The technical drawings can evolve with the project, supporting every phase from initial concept through detailed planning.

Sustainability assessments: solar analyses, building performance simulations

Full analysis of building performance based on its location and orientation. The results are presented in detailed diagrams showing optimal areas for a photovoltaic installation, accounting for both self-shading effects and any shadows cast by neighboring buildings.
