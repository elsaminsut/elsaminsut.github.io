Services

There are different ways in which I can help companies or individual customers. Although my focus has been lately in the solar business, if you have any ideas related to the automation of anything CAD-related, I'll be happy to hear about them.

Planning services

Creation of optimized module layouts for solar building skins, finding the best choice based on cost-efficiency, energy yield, and visual appeal.

Solar potential analyses

Full analysis of building performance based on its location and orientation. The results show optimal areas for a PV installation, accounting for any shading effects due to neighboring buildings.

Feasibility studies

A comprehensive building analysis that evaluates each façade's potential for a PV installation. The study delivers key metrics including energy generation performance and ROI to determine suitable façade locations.

Automated tools

Automatic creation of technical drawings that comply with datasheets and manufacturing requirements, including plans for large installations with thousands of accurate module drawings produced at once.
