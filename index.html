Hello! I'm Elsa, a Computational Designer and Engineer with over 10 years of professional experience.

While I've worked for companies across different engineering and construction fields, my focus has been on optimizing processes in the CAD environment. In recent years, I've specialized in sustainability and solar active facades, conducting detailed environmental assessments and feasibility studies. I can help customers evaluate their buildings' potential for solar installations through comprehensive analyses of energy performance, ROI calculations, and solar benefit analyses to help maximize environmental and financial returns.

If you have a project in mind and would like to explore how we could work together, I'd love to hear from you! Check out my services page to learn more about how I can support your project.
